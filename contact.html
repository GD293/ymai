Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I get involved as a volunteer?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities. You can send us a mail to indicate interest in volunteering opportunities available.

What are the eligibility requirements for your programs?

Our programs are generally open to youth aged 13-24. Specific eligibility requirements vary by program. Please visit our Programs page for detailed information or send us a mail.

How can I make a donation to support your work?

Please send us a mail for your donations or corporate partnerships. All donations are tax-deductible, and we provide receipts for your records.
